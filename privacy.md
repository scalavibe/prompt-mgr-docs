Privacy Policy
Last Updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prompt Mgr ("Extension"), developed by Shev4uk ("Developer"), handles your data. Your privacy is a core priority, and the Extension is designed to keep your personal data entirely under your own control.

1. No Collection of Personal Information
The Extension does not collect, store, track, or transmit any of your personal data, credentials, browsing history, or the contents of your prompts to the Developer or any third-party servers. All data you create remains strictly on your device or within your private Google account synchronization.

1.1. Data Retention
Since we do not collect or store your data on any external servers, we do not retain your data. Your data is retained on your local device or your Google Account until you choose to delete the Extension or clear your browser storage.

2. Data Storage and Google Chrome Sync
Local Storage: All data you save—such as prompt names, prompt texts, folder structures, and settings—is stored locally on your device within your Google Chrome browser profile.
Chrome Sync: If you are signed in to Google Chrome and have synchronization enabled, your saved prompts and settings are synced across your devices using Google's Chrome Sync service. This data transmission occurs directly between your browser and Google's servers. The Developer has no access to, visibility of, or control over this synchronized data.
3. Permissions Used and Why
To function correctly, the Extension requests the following permissions from your browser:

storage: Used to save, edit, and sync your prompts and settings locally and via Google Chrome Sync.
contextMenus: Used to display the "Prompt Mgr" submenu when you right-click on web pages, allowing you to quickly save selected text or select prompts to paste.
scripting & activeTab: Used to securely inject the Alt+A quick paste menu and insert your template texts directly into active text inputs on the page you are viewing. The Extension does not monitor, read, or collect browsing history or data from other tabs.
4. Third-Party Analytics and Advertising
The Extension does not contain any analytics trackers, diagnostic reporting tools, or advertisements. No tracking identifiers are generated, and no usage statistics are shared with third parties.

5. Security of Stored Data
Because your data is stored locally in your Chrome profile and synced via Google Chrome Sync, the security of your prompts depends on:

The physical and software security of your local device.
The security of your Google Account (e.g., using strong passwords and multi-factor authentication).
Warning: You should avoid storing sensitive data (such as login passwords, credit card numbers, or encryption keys) as prompts, as the Extension is not designed to function as an encrypted credential vault.
6. Children's Privacy
The Extension does not collect any data, and is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy
The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be updated directly in the Extension's distribution channels or documentation. Your continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes your agreement to the updated policy.
